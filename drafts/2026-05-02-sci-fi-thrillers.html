Killer Whale (2026)

Starring: Virginia Gardner, Mel Jarnson, Mitchell Hope

It's feeding time.

Killer Whale tells the story of two friends, Maddie and Trish, who find themselves trapped in a remote lagoon with a deadly killer whale. This claustrophobic setting creates a sense of tension and dread that's reminiscent of Jaws.

The film's use of practical effects brings the whale to life, making its attacks both terrifying and believable. However, some viewers may find the plot twists predictable, with some scenes feeling overly convenient or contrived.

Fans of Deep Blue Sea and The Shallows will love the aquatic horror elements of Killer Whale. The film's themes of survival and friendship will also resonate with viewers who enjoy character-driven dramas.

PickMyBinge Verdict: 5.6/10

Kill Code (2026)

Starring: Franzi Schissler, Frank Grillo, Harvey Keitel

Judge. Executioner. Freedom.

Kill Code explores the theme of corruption and redemption in a dystopian future. The film's world-building is detailed and immersive, with a complex web of characters and motivations that drive the plot forward.

The film's use of social commentary critiques the exploitation of power and the dangers of unchecked authority. The cast delivers strong performances, bringing depth and nuance to their characters. However, some viewers may find the pacing uneven, with some scenes feeling rushed or convenient.

Viewers who enjoy The Purge and District 9 will appreciate the dark, gritty tone of Kill Code. The film's themes of resistance and rebellion will also resonate with fans of The Hunger Games and V for Vendetta.

PickMyBinge Verdict: 6.0/10

Disclosure Day (2026)

Starring: Emily Blunt, Josh O'Connor, Colin Firth

We deserve to know.

Disclosure Day poses a thought-provoking question: what if we're not alone in the universe? This mystery-driven narrative explores the implications of first contact and the human condition.

The film's use of visuals and sound design creates a sense of unease and wonder. The cast delivers powerful performances, bringing depth and emotion to their characters. However, some viewers may find the pacing slow, with some scenes feeling overly introspective or philosophical.

Fans of Close Encounters of the Third Kind and Signs will love the first-contact elements of Disclosure Day. The film's themes of discovery and exploration will also resonate with viewers who enjoy Interstellar and The Martian.
