Your privacy is important to us

Last Updated: April 12, 2026

Welcome to PickMyBinge! This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our movie recommendation service. Please read this carefully. If you do not agree with these terms, please do not access the site.

1. Information We Collect

Personal Data

We may collect personally identifiable information that you voluntarily provide when you:

  • Register an account
  • Subscribe to our newsletter
  • Complete surveys or provide feedback
  • Contact us with inquiries

Personal information may include your name, email address, and movie preferences.

Derived Data

Information our servers automatically collect when you access the site, such as your IP address, browser type, operating system, access times, and pages viewed.

Movie Preference Data

We collect information about the movies you like, dislike, rate, and add to your watchlist to provide personalised recommendations.

2. Use of Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Create and manage your account

We never sell your personal information to third parties. Your movie preferences and personal data are used solely to enhance your experience on PickMyBinge.

We use cookies, web beacons, and similar tracking technologies to collect information about your interactions with our website. Cookies help us:

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may limit some features of our service.

4. Third-Party Services

We may use third-party service providers to:

  • Analyse how our website is used

These third parties have access to your information only to perform tasks on our behalf and are obligated not to disclose or use it for any other purpose.

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information. No method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure, but we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your data.

6. Children's Privacy

PickMyBinge is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13. If you become aware that a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the information below.
